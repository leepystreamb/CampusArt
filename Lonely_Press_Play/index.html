Artist Statement

Lonely Press Play is a photo series that features the glimpse of connection between human and machine, as well as the connectedness between the two frames: The photographed is being captured in the photo frame; and in conjunction, the frame they are engaging at. The machine, whether it is my camera, or their phone, constituted an interconnected isolation, the photographed being precluded in the attachment to their frame from the environment, and I am connected to their oblivion, isolated from my own surroundings.
