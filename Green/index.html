GreenVIDEO + ARTIST STATEMENT

Green is a collection of clips that captures the greenery in a city. The greenery as part of nature itself, immobile and stationary, becomes the living part of nature, human activities and city infrastructures. Their relationship in bargaining between barriers and space show a still battlefield in harmony. This is an appreciation I have for the trees, bushes, flowers and weeds, for them to make my home town a dear place to me.
