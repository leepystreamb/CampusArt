Elégia

Author: Zoltán Huszárik

Date of Creation: 1966


This film is the main inspiration for my graduation project "Elegy in Time". This film has an excellent visual narrative to show something passing. And in my project, it is about a mourning process for the definite death of Hong Kong.

Web Collage. Artwork Site.


Cameron’s World

Author: Cameron Askin

Date of Creation: 2015


Cameron’s World is a web collage of GIF images gathered and archived from 1994 to 2009. As an amateur internet lore historian myself, I am inspired to examine, document and preserve the many ephemeral chapters on the internet. This work is such a good example to make a resurgence of the glorious days of WWW 1.0 and GeoCities. It revived forgotten internet culture and aesthetics. As someone who grew up with these sites, I am glad to see that a whole new population gets to see the resurgence of the glorious days of the Internet and hope to do the same in my future projects.

Photography. Image source.


念香港人的舊 (Portrait of Hong Kong)

Author: Ho Fan

Date of Creation: 2017


Portrait of Hong Kong is a photography collection that Ho Fan took in Hong Kong during the 1950s to 1960s. For my photography works, I hope to reflect as effectively as Ho Fan the authentic lives and cityscape. Through his works, it enlightens me to develop the sensibility of capturing the normalcy that will eventually fade out in a fast changing city or landscape. Especially under a definitive political instability in my home Hong Kong, it has become an urgency to preserve the fragments of my own fading normalcy.

Photography. Image source.


The Ballad of Sexual Dependency

Author: Nan Goldin

Date of Creation: 2017


The Ballad of Sexual Dependency is a slideshow that showcase the nightlife, the intimate moments of sex, drugs, and even violence. It documented the ostracised communities in the 1980s: the fleeting high from drugs, the underground drag scenes, and a glimpse of daily life of some now lost lives due to AIDs pandemic. I admire the honesty of Goldin’s work and it is and always will be a principle I have for photography.

The Ballad of Sexual Dependency

The Ballad of Sexual Dependency est un diaporama qui expose la vie nocturne, les moments intimes de sexe, de drogue et de violence. J'admire l'honnêteté du travail de Goldin et c'est et ce sera toujours un principe pour mon projet de photographie.

La Société du Spectacle est un essai révolutionnaire qui avait prévu la société contemporaine et son rapport avec la technologie. Ce livre a suscité mon intérêt pour l'étude de la théorie critique, et en mettant les nombreux aspects de l'internet et de la technologie à l’examen sous un prisme critique.

Control and Freedom: Power and Paranoia in the Age of Fiber Optics

Author: Wendy Hui Kyong Chun

Date of Creation: 2006


The book dissects thoroughly how to reframe the perspective regarding computers and internet as a new found tool for society to ‘gain’ freedom and liberation, and how much control is involved in this ‘freedom’, how much the seeming freedom is lost when technology is read under a feminist lens. It has broadened my horizon and influenced me to pursue researching on New Media.

Control and Freedom: Power and Paranoia in the Age of Fiber Optics

Auteur: Wendy Hui Kyong Chun

Date of Création: 2006


Le livre explique en détail comment recadrer la perspective des ordinateurs et de l'internet en tant que nouvel outil permettant à la société de "gagner" la liberté et la libération, et dans quelle mesure le contrôle est impliqué par cette "liberté". Dans certains cas, l'apparente liberté est perdue lorsque la technologie est lue à travers un angle féministe. Ce livre m’a incité à poursuivre des recherches sur les nouveaux médias grâce à ses pensées provocantes et son encadrement créatif.

Hope

Author: George Frederic Watts

Date of Creation: 1886, 1886-1895


For me, this painting represents the essence of painting. It conveys a sentiment that is lost but reminiscent, something in peril but persistent. Her frail posture yet to resemble any defeatedness, instead a strong presence of power, the power of hope. The painting has always resonated with me in my life and art creation when dealing with political uncertainty.

Hope

Auteur: George Frederic Watts

Date of Création: 1886, 1886-1895


Ce tableau, pour moi, représente l'essence de la peinture. Il transmet un sentiment qui est perdu, en même temps, une grande présence de pouvoir qui persiste. La posture frêle de la femme n’a pas l’air de défaite, au contraire, elle possède l’éspoir: l’une chose que je me rapelle de n’y pas oublier, l’une chose que je ne peux pas me permettre de perdre dans la vie.
