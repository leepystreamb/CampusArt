UntitledVIDEO + ARTIST STATEMENT

This video piece is an attempt to explore a large infrastructure and its sense of space. The camera targets different parts of a vast space and folds it into one layer of ‘tableau’, a cross-section of precision and confusion within an architecture, by obscuring the visible space and entailing the invisible.

DISCOVERIES

In reviewing the work, one thing I find interesting is that the airport as an architectural statement of modernity, like many other modern buildings, often has this sleek and stainless texture in it. And for that exact reason, at least for me, looking at these clear structures at a certain angle, the sense of space is obscured by it. In this case, the camera has another sleek and reflective layer compiled on the same texture.

In terms of adapting the internal montage system, I wanted to have interlaced layers in it to represent the layers in materials. Like a layered cake, with the sponge cake and the filling, the sponge cake being the obscured shot with layers of reflections , filling being the flat and clear shot of what exactly is happening inside the building.
